About DRC

Daily Remit Coin (DRC) is a next-generation digital asset built to power fast, low-cost, and secure global transactions. By leveraging blockchain technology, DRC eliminates the inefficiencies of traditional remittance systems while expanding into a scalable ecosystem of DeFi, NFTs, staking, and decentralized governance.

DRC is designed for real-world utility and long-term growth, aiming to become a cornerstone of borderless finance.
