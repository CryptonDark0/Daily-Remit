How to Buy DRC

DRC is currently available on decentralized exchanges (DEXs) on the BNB Smart Chain. Follow these steps to purchase:

1

Set Up a Wallet

Download MetaMask or Trust Wallet and connect to BNB Smart Chain network.

2

Get BNB

Buy BNB from any exchange and transfer it to your wallet for gas fees and swapping.

3

Go to PancakeSwap

Visit PancakeSwap, connect your wallet, and paste the DRC contract address in the token field.

4

Swap BNB for DRC

Set your slippage tolerance (recommended 5-10%), enter the amount, and confirm the swap.

5

Add DRC to Wallet

In your wallet, go to "Add Token", paste the contract address, and DRC will appear in your portfolio.

About DRC

What is Daily Remit Coin?

Daily Remit Coin (DRC) is a next-generation digital asset built to power fast, low-cost, and secure global transactions. By leveraging blockchain technology, DRC eliminates the inefficiencies of traditional remittance systems while expanding into a scalable ecosystem of DeFi, NFTs, staking, and decentralized governance.

Designed for both real-world utility and long-term growth, DRC aims to become a cornerstone of borderless finance.

Fast & Low-Cost

Near-instant transactions with significantly reduced costs compared to traditional systems.

Deflationary Burn

1% of every transaction is permanently burned, reducing supply over time.

Secure & Transparent

Built on BNB Smart Chain with audited smart contracts and on-chain verifiability.
