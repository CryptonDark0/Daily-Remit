DRC Whitepaper

Daily Remit Coin (DRC)

Whitepaper v1.0 — 2025

1. Executive Summary

Daily Remit Coin (DRC) is a BEP-20 token deployed on the BNB Smart Chain, designed to revolutionize global remittance by providing fast, low-cost, and secure cross-border transactions. With a deflationary mechanism built into every transaction, DRC combines real-world utility with sustainable tokenomics.

2. Problem Statement

Traditional remittance services charge 5-10% in fees, take 2-5 business days for settlement, and exclude millions of unbanked individuals globally.

3. Solution

DRC leverages the BNB Smart Chain to offer near-instant transactions at a fraction of traditional costs. The 4% transaction fee (1% Marketing, 1% Development, 1% Liquidity, 1% Burn) creates a self-sustaining ecosystem.
